    Questions, answered

    Do I need to know anything to start?

    No background needed. Archives starts at the beginning, with the story of Nuh, and builds forward through the eras one short story at a time.

    How much time does it take?

    About five minutes a day. One story, one question. It fits a coffee break, a commute, or the few minutes before bed.

    Is it accurate?

    Yes. Every story is carefully researched and sourced from real history, so you can trust that what you are learning actually happened.

    Is it free?

    You can start for free. A subscription unlocks the full library and learning tools when you are ready for more.

    Who is it for?

    Anyone curious about Islamic history, from complete beginners to lifelong readers, plus parents who want better screen time and classrooms.
